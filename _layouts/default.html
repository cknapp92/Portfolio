Idea Behind The Project

To learn and hone new skills I am acquiring in programming, to show my commitment to this task, I decided to test myself: Make a new webpage every day for the next 111 days. From the beginning of August to the project's conclusion, I'll be displaying a new page I've made in the course of a day. It may display a new feature in JavaScript I've learned, it may be my replication of a website I like, it will be an attempt to put what I'm learning every day into practice. On top of sharing all that I create on this website, I'll be writing a blog that covers every step of the way.
